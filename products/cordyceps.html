B2B supply guide

Cordyceps militaris
wholesale supply, explained.

Everything a supplement-brand sourcing manager needs to know before placing a cordyceps brief — supply formats, lab actives, certifications, lead times.

Whole fruiting body vs mycelium-on-grain — why it matters

The majority of bulk “cordyceps” supplied into the US and EU supplement market is actually mycelium grown on grain (rice, oats or sorghum) — milled together with the substrate and sold as a single powder. It is cheaper to produce, but the finished material is dominated by grain starch and contains significantly lower cordycepin and beta-glucan than the fruiting body itself. Many brands have moved off mycelium-on-grain in the last 36 months as label claims and HPLC scrutiny have tightened. Good Mushroom supplies only the whole fruiting body — the orange-amber stroma harvested from the substrate and dried — milled to mesh-100 powder for capsules and blends, or extracted 8:1–20:1 dual-extract. No grain carrier. No starch dilution. The cordycepin and beta-glucan numbers on our COA are real ingredient values, not substrate-diluted figures. If you are reformulating off a mycelium-on-grain ingredient, expect a 3–8x uplift in actives per gram compared to typical mycelium-on-grain spec sheets.

Cordyceps militaris vs Cordyceps sinensis — what we supply

We supply Cordyceps militaris only — the lab-cultivable species. We do not handle wild Cordyceps sinensis (CITES-listed, ecologically restricted and frequently adulterated). Cultivated C. militaris matches or exceeds wild C. sinensis on cordycepin content in published comparative studies, which is why most reputable supplement and nutraceutical brands have moved their ingredient supply to cultivated militaris over the past 10 years. Every batch we ship is grown indoors on a sterile substrate by a verified growing partner under our documented sourcing protocol.

Cordyceps wholesale supply formats

We supply cordyceps in three primary formats. Dried whole fruiting body — orange-amber, intact, low-moisture (under 10%), graded for visual consistency, ideal for buyers who run their own extraction. Mesh-100 cordyceps powder — milled from dried fruiting body, free-flowing, supplement-grade. Dual-extract powder, 8:1 to 20:1 — water plus ethanol extraction, spray-dried, mesh 80–120, beta-glucan and cordycepin verified, formulated to supplement-ready specifications. Cordyceps wholesale MOQ is 10 kg for dried fruiting body, 25 kg for powder and 5 kg for extract on the first PO.

Cordycepin and adenosine — verified per batch

Every batch ships with an HPLC actives report on cordycepin and adenosine. Cordycepin is typically up to 12.5 mg/g across our verified supplier base. Adenosine is approximately ~1.5 mg/g. Both sit well above the trace levels typical of mycelium-on-grain material. We report the actual lot value on every COA rather than a marketing target, so what you see on the certificate is what ships. If you're running a finished-product label claim, tell us the minimum you need and we'll confirm against current lot data before you commit to a PO.

Cordyceps buyers — who orders this ingredient

The most common buyers for our cordyceps bulk supply are nootropic and energy supplement brands (capsules, gummies, energy stick-packs), sports-nutrition private-label manufacturers, mushroom-coffee and functional-beverage brands, traditional Chinese medicine and Ayurveda-adjacent finished-product companies, and pharmaceutical excipient buyers running their own downstream extraction. We work with first-time importers and multi-container repeat buyers on the same documentation and lab process.

Why India — a non-China cordyceps source for diversifying buyers

Post-2020 supply-chain diversification has pushed most US, EU, UK and Gulf supplement brands to add a non-China origin for every critical herbal ingredient. Cordyceps is one of the highest-priority categories on those diversification lists. India has emerged as the strongest alternative for cultivated C. militaris on three counts: price — our FOB India quote is typically 20–30% below an equivalent Chinese FOB Guangzhou price for whole fruiting body powder; documentation — NABL COA and phyto-sanitary certificate on every shipment, with FSSAI and APEDA registration currently in process, in a language and format that US/EU brokers and customs already handle; and traceability — small, named growing partners under a documented sourcing protocol, not an anonymous wholesale lot. If “reduce China exposure” is a 2026 procurement objective, this is one of the cleanest single-line items to swap.

Documentation, certifications and export support

Every cordyceps shipment includes NABL COA, phyto-sanitary certificate, commercial invoice and batch-traceable packing list — with FSSAI and APEDA export registration currently in process. Optional organic, kosher and halal certifications available on request via certified partners (adds 2–3 weeks). Air freight for orders under 100 kg, sea freight for larger. Our team handles customs questions before your broker raises them.
