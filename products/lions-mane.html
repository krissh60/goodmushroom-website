Hericium erinaceus — what we supply

We supply indoor-cultivated Hericium erinaceus from Indian growing partners. Full-formed white fruiting bodies (not mycelium-on-grain), dried under controlled conditions; the moisture specification is confirmed by product route, then either supplied whole or milled to mesh-100 powder. We also supply an 8:1 dual extract powder — water plus ethanol extraction, spray-dried, supplement-ready. Lion's Mane wholesale orders start at 20 kg dried fruiting body or 5 kg extract on the first PO; mesh powder MOQ is 25 kg.

Hericenones vs erinacines — verified per batch

Two distinct active compound families matter for Lion's Mane ingredient buyers. Hericenones are associated with the fruiting-body fraction; testing can be commissioned to the agreed specification. Erinacines are found in the mycelium and can be sourced as a separate extract if your formulation calls for both fractions. Most supplement brands sourcing Lion's Mane for nootropic and cognitive-health finished products want the fruiting body (hericenones), but we can structure dual-fraction supply for brands running a science-backed positioning.

Lion's Mane buyers — who orders this ingredient

Our Lion's Mane wholesale supply is most commonly ordered by nootropic and cognitive-health supplement brands, functional-coffee and mushroom-coffee private-label manufacturers, capsule and gummy formulators, clinical-grade nutraceutical companies running label claims on hericenone content, and Ayurveda-adjacent brands integrating Lion's Mane into adaptogenic stacks. Hericium erinaceus is one of the highest-growth functional mushroom ingredients globally and Indian cultivated supply can be evaluated against the buyer specification and testing plan.

What buyers should check before ordering Lion's Mane

The key buying decision is fruiting body, mycelium or dual-fraction supply. Fruiting body material is usually selected for visible mushroom identity, dried powder and hericenone positioning. Mycelium-based material may be requested where erinacines are part of the formulation, but it must be clearly specified. Buyers should also confirm drying quality, colour, aroma, extract ratio, beta-glucan target, contaminant testing and whether the shipment is meant for capsules, coffee, gummies or downstream extraction.

Where to sell Lion's Mane mushrooms in India

Lion's Mane growers in India can sell to gourmet restaurants, health-food brands, supplement formulators, dehydrated-food processors and B2B ingredient buyers. Good Mushroom is relevant when the grower can produce repeatable, clean, white fruiting bodies and wants structured supply terms. We review growing method, drying control, monthly capacity, current buyer route and whether the material is best sold whole, powdered or extracted.

Documentation, certifications and export

Every Lion's Mane shipment ships with accredited partner-lab COA where commissioned (hericenones, beta-glucan, heavy metals by ICP-MS, full microbiology, pesticide residue), phyto-sanitary certificate, commercial invoice and batch-level packing list — FSSAI and APEDA documentation as registration completes. USDA NOP, EU Organic, NPOP, kosher and halal certifications available on request. Air freight under 100 kg; sea freight for larger orders.
