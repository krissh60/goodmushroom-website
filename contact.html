Before You Write

Quick Answers

🌍

Can you ship to my country?

We export worldwide. Just mention your country in the form and we'll confirm shipping routes, timelines, and any import restrictions we're aware of.

📦

What's the minimum order?

MOQ varies by product. Typically 10–50 kg for dried mushrooms, 5 kg for extracts. We offer smaller sample shipments for first-time buyers who want to test quality.

🌾

I'm just a small farmer — can I still sell?

Absolutely. We work with farmers of all sizes. If your quality is good, we want to hear from you. We can help you scale up too if you're interested.
