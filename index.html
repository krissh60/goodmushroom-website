Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything buyers and sellers need to know before working with us.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for export?

+
MOQ varies by product. Typically, dried mushrooms start at 50kg, extracts at 5kg, and fresh mushrooms at 100kg per order. For first-time buyers, we offer small trial/sample shipments so you can test quality before committing to large orders.

Which countries do you export to?

+
We export to the USA, UK, EU countries, UAE, Japan, South Korea, Australia, Canada, and many more. If your country is not listed, please contact us — we are happy to explore new markets with you.

Are your mushrooms certified organic?

+
Many of our partner farms follow organic growing practices. We are in the process of obtaining APEDA organic certification for select products. Please specify your certification requirements in your enquiry and we will match you with certified sources.

Can you provide private label / white-label packaging?

+
Yes. We offer white-label packaging for supplement brands and distributors. You provide your label artwork, we handle production and packaging in your branded materials — minimum order quantities apply.

I am a mushroom farmer. How do I sell to you?

+
Simply fill in our Seller Registration form or call us. We'll schedule a visit to your farm, assess quality, and discuss an offtake agreement. We buy multiple species: Oyster, Shiitake, Lion's Mane, Cordyceps, Button, Reishi, and more.

What certifications do you hold or plan to obtain?

+
We are registered / in process of registration with FSSAI, APEDA, and hold an IEC (Import Export Code). Our products are tested at third-party NABL-accredited laboratories. We are also pursuing GMP certification for our extract processing unit.

How long does it take to ship from India to my country?

+
Air freight typically takes 3–7 days. Sea freight takes 15–30 days depending on the destination. Dried products can ship by sea, while fresh mushrooms require air freight with appropriate cold-chain packaging.

Do you offer samples before bulk orders?

+
Yes. We provide paid samples (cost + shipping) of most products so you can evaluate quality before placing bulk orders. Sample quantities are typically 100g–500g depending on the product type.
