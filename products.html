Cordyceps militaris — bulk supply for nutraceutical brands

Lab-cultivated Cordyceps militaris from Indian growing partners, supplied as dried fruiting body, mesh-100 powder and 8:1–20:1 dual extracts. Cordycepin (5–8%) and adenosine (0.3–0.6%) verified by HPLC on every batch, NABL-accredited COA included. Cordyceps wholesale orders start at 10 kg dried or 5 kg extract on the first PO. Typical buyers: nootropic and energy supplement brands, capsule formulators, sports-nutrition private-label manufacturers. Cordyceps militaris ingredient supply specifications →

Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) — wholesale ingredient supply

Indoor-cultivated Lion's Mane from India — white, fully-formed fruiting bodies dried under controlled humidity, plus 8:1 dual extract powder. Both hericenones (fruiting-body fraction) and erinacines (mycelium fraction) verified by HPLC per batch. Lion's Mane wholesale MOQ is 20 kg dried or 5 kg extract. Used by nootropic supplement brands, cognitive-health formulators, functional coffee and mushroom-coffee private-label manufacturers, and clinical-grade nutraceutical companies. Reishi / Ganoderma lucidum — extract, dual extract & spore oil

Cultivated Ganoderma lucidum from Indian farms, supplied as whole sliced fruiting body, mesh-100 powder, 10:1 dual-extract powder, broken-shell spore powder and supercritical CO₂ reishi spore oil. Triterpenes (>35%) and beta-glucan verified by HPLC per batch. Reishi wholesale MOQ is 50 kg dried / 5 kg extract / 1 kg spore oil. Typical buyers: immune-support supplement brands, longevity formulators, pharmaceutical excipient buyers, premium Ayurveda brands. Reishi (Ganoderma lucidum) ingredient supply →

Shiitake (Lentinula edodes) — donko & koshin grades, bulk supply

Premium shiitake from Indian growing partners — Donko thick-cap and Koshin flat-cap grades, supplied as fresh cold-chain (regional markets), dried whole, sliced and mesh-100 powder. Lentinan present, ergosterol verified, beta-glucan 20–35% reported per batch. Shiitake wholesale MOQ is 50 kg dried or 100 kg / week fresh. Buyers include functional-food manufacturers, gourmet retail importers, supplement brands sourcing β-glucan actives, and food-service distributors. Shiitake bulk ingredient supply →

Dual-extract mushroom powders — 8:1 to 20:1, supplement-ready

How buying from us works

Plain-English
buyer guide.

Everything a sourcing manager needs to know before sending a brief. If you'd rather skip ahead, the buyer enquiry form takes about 90 seconds.

Who buys from Good Mushroom Pvt Ltd

Our typical buyers are supplement and nutraceutical brands sourcing actives for capsules, gummies and powders; functional food and beverage companies; specialty grocers and restaurant groups sourcing dried culinary mushrooms; private-label and OEM manufacturers running white-label brands; and herbal product brands sourcing AYUSH-licensed Ayurvedic herbs. We work with first-time importers as comfortably as we work with multi-container repeat buyers — the documentation, lab testing and shipping process is the same either way.

Minimum order quantity (MOQ) by product

MOQ depends on the product format. For dual-extract powders and reishi spore oil the MOQ is 5 kg per SKU. For cordyceps militaris dried fruiting body it is 10 kg. For Lion's Mane dried it is 20 kg. For ashwagandha powder it is 25 kg. For most other dried mushrooms (shiitake, reishi, oyster, button) the MOQ is 50 kg. For fresh cold-chain product we ship 100–200 kg per week to nearby export markets. Mixed-pallet orders across SKUs are allowed so newer buyers can hit MOQ without committing to a single product.

Pricing model — how we quote

Every quote includes FOB Indian port pricing (Mumbai, Delhi or Bangalore depending on product) and CIF landed pricing to your destination port. We hold pricing valid for 14 days against confirmed PO, and quarterly contracts are available for buyers committing to a monthly schedule. There are no broker spreads, commission percentages or third-party markups between you and the farm. You pay the farm-gate price plus our QC + processing + logistics — disclosed line by line on request for buyers on annual contracts.

Lab testing — what every batch comes with

Every Good Mushroom batch ships with a NABL-accredited Certificate of Analysis. The COA reports actives by HPLC (cordycepin, hericenones, triterpenes, β-glucan, withanolides — whichever apply to your product), heavy metals by ICP-MS (lead, arsenic, cadmium, mercury), full microbiological panel (TPC, yeast and mould, E. coli, salmonella), pesticide residue screening, and mycotoxin testing where applicable. We can share recent batch COAs at quote stage so your QA team can verify against your in-house spec before you commit to a paid sample.

Sample process and lead times

Paid samples — 100 g to 500 g depending on the product — ship by air, typically arriving in 5–7 days from dispatch. Sample cost is creditable in full against your first commercial order. For full commercial orders, lead time runs roughly 2–4 weeks for production and packaging, then 1 week air or 4–6 weeks sea depending on destination. Status updates are sent on WhatsApp every Thursday from PO confirmation through dispatch.

Export documentation we provide

We handle the full export documentation set on every shipment: phyto-sanitary certificate, FSSAI clearance, APEDA registration certificate, commercial invoice, packing list with batch-level traceability, NABL COA, and bill of lading or air waybill. Optional certifications — USDA Organic, EU Organic, kosher, halal, non-GMO Project Verified — are routed through our certified partners on request, with 2–3 weeks added lead time and per-batch certification cost disclosed upfront.

Certifications held by Good Mushroom Pvt Ltd

Good Mushroom Private Limited holds FSSAI registration for food handling, APEDA registration as an agricultural exporter, AYUSH licensing for our herbal extracts and Ayurvedic herbs (Ashwagandha, Tulsi, Brahmi, Shatavari), and operates a HACCP-compatible processing facility. We are phyto-sanitary ready for every export shipment.
