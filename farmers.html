Quality Standards

What We Look For

Our international buyers have strict requirements. Here's what we need from farmers to meet export-grade standards.

💧

Moisture Control

Dried mushrooms must meet moisture specs (typically <12%). Fresh mushrooms should be harvested at optimal maturity.

🚫

No Pesticide Residue

We test every batch. Pesticide-free growing methods are preferred and will fetch premium prices from our buyers.

🦠

Clean Substrate

Substrate should be free of contamination. We prefer straw, sawdust, or grain-based growing for culinary mushrooms.

📏

Consistent Grading

Uniform size and colour grading is preferred. We can assist with basic post-harvest sorting if needed.

📦

Clean Packaging

Deliver in food-safe bags or crates. We'll provide packaging guidelines after onboarding.

📍

Location Flexible

We source from across India — Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and more. Distance is not a barrier.

Common Questions

Farmer FAQs

What is the minimum quantity you buy?

+
We work with farmers of all sizes. For initial trial, even 5–10 kg is fine. Once we establish quality and trust, we scale up to regular bulk orders.

Do you only buy from Himachal Pradesh?

+
No, we source from across India. We have suppliers in HP, Uttarakhand, Punjab, Maharashtra, Karnataka and more. We'll arrange pickup or logistics.

How soon do you pay after delivery?

+
We pay within 7 days of delivery and quality verification. For long-term partners, we can arrange advance payments for large orders.

I'm new to cultivation. Can you help?

+
Yes. We provide growing guidance for Cordyceps, Shiitake, and Lion's Mane cultivation. We can connect you with our network of experienced growers too.
