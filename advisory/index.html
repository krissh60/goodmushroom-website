Frequently asked questions

Clear boundaries before you enquire.

Do you provide mushroom cultivation training?

No. Cohort training is not part of the current advisory model. The work is focused on a defined commercial question, readiness gap or project-report scope.

Can you guarantee a buyer, loan, subsidy or profit?

No. We can explain buyer-facing requirements and project assumptions, but we do not guarantee commercial outcomes, approvals, licences, yield or profitability.

When should I choose a fixed report rather than a qualification call?

Request a fixed report when your decision matches its published scope. Use the paid qualification call when you need a DPR, custom project advisory or help deciding which service fits.

Is the qualification-call fee credited?

Yes. The ₹499 fee is credited against a DPR purchased within seven days of the call.
