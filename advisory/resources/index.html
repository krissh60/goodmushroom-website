Public reference points

Use primary sources for technical and regulatory questions.

Good Mushroom Advisory can help frame the commercial question, but specialist information should come from the relevant authority or professional. For scientific mushroom-research reference, start with ICAR’s Directorate of Mushroom Research listing. For current food, labelling, packaging and claims material, review the FSSAI regulations hub.
