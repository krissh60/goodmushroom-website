Our story

Founded by two friends in Himachal · incorporated as Good Mushroom Pvt Ltd in 2026.

After years of seeing buyers shipped inconsistent functional mushroom ingredients from India, Krish and Anmol started Good Mushroom Private Limited to build something different — a sourcing & supply company that owns the procurement, quality and documentation chain end-to-end.

We're an Indian company, officially incorporated in 2026 and registered as Good Mushroom Pvt Ltd, headquartered in Mandi, Himachal Pradesh. We combine supplier relationships with operational understanding of procurement, logistics and supply-chain execution. Every batch is lab-tested. Every contract is direct. Every shipment is documented. Every buyer gets exactly what they ordered.

What we stand for

Three things,
repeated.

The rules we wrote ourselves when we started. We still hold to them — they're why our buyers reorder and our farmers stay.

01 · Principle

Direct, never brokered

We buy from farms, not aggregators. We sell to buyers, not other exporters. The chain is two links long. Always.

02 · Principle

Lab-verified, every batch

NABL-accredited labs. HPLC for actives, ICP-MS for heavy metals, micro panel, pesticide screen. COA in every box.

03 · Principle

Transparent pricing

Farmers see the export price. Buyers see the farm price. The spread covers QC, processing, logistics — and is the same for everyone.

04 · Promise

If it doesn't match, we replace

Spec mismatch? Send it back. We replace, no questions, no broker fights. Our reputation is built one batch at a time.
