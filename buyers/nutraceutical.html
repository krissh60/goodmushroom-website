Lab-verified actives on every batch

Nutraceutical brands run label claims, and label claims require an upstream supplier who can support them. Every batch of our mushroom ingredient supply ships with a NABL-accredited COA reporting actives by HPLC — cordycepin and adenosine for cordyceps, hericenones for lion's mane, triterpenes and beta-glucan for reishi, withanolides for ashwagandha, lentinan and beta-glucan for shiitake. Heavy metals by ICP-MS, full microbiological panel, pesticide residue and mycotoxins screened to international supplement-industry thresholds.

Supplement-ready formats and packaging

Our mushroom ingredient supply formats are built around nutraceutical manufacturing workflows. Dual-extract powders at 8:1 to 20:1 strengths, spray-dried, mesh 80–120, free-flowing for direct encapsulation, sachet filling or gummy formulation. Mesh-100 mushroom powders for buyers running their own downstream extraction or formulation. Food-grade liners, foil-laminated drums and nitrogen-purged packs for actives-sensitive ingredients. Private-label retail packaging available on contract.

Documentation and certifications

NABL-accredited lab partners for batch testing, HACCP-aligned processing facility. FSSAI, APEDA and AYUSH registrations are currently in process. Optional USDA Organic, EU Organic, NPOP, JAS, kosher and halal certifications routed through our certified partners on request — typical 2–3 weeks added lead time and per-batch certification cost disclosed upfront.

Sample, sample, then production

Most nutraceutical brands run a 1–2 sample cycle on a new ingredient supplier — different lots, side-by-side comparative assays — before placing a production PO. We support that workflow. 100 g to 500 g paid samples ship by air, typically arriving 5–7 days from dispatch with full NABL COA. Sample cost is creditable in full against your first commercial order. We don't push for production POs before your QA team has validated supply against your in-house specifications.

Common nutraceutical ingredient orders

Most-ordered combinations for nutraceutical sourcing teams: cordyceps militaris dual-extract powder for energy and nootropic stacks, lion's mane 8:1 extract for cognitive-health and nootropic formulations, reishi 10:1 dual extract and reishi spore oil for immune-support and longevity stacks, ashwagandha root extract standardized to withanolides for adaptogenic and stress-support products. Mixed-pallet orders across SKUs welcome — most first-time nutraceutical buyers order 3–5 SKUs together on the first commercial PO.
