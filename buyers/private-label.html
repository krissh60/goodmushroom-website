Custom mushroom blends — your formulation

Send us your blend specification and we'll build it. Common private-label blends we run: 10-mushroom adaptogenic stack (cordyceps, lion's mane, reishi, chaga, turkey tail, maitake, shiitake, tremella, agaricus, poria), focus & nootropic blend (lion's mane, cordyceps, bacopa, ashwagandha), immune blend (reishi, chaga, turkey tail, agaricus blazei), energy blend (cordyceps, ashwagandha, eleuthero). Your ratios, your target actives, your finished-product label claims. Testing scope can be specified for the blend lot before dispatch.

COA issued in your private-label brand name

Standard COA issuance is in Good Mushroom Private Limited's name. For private-label and OEM buyers we issue the NABL-accredited Certificate of Analysis in your agreed lot reference — downstream label and traceability requirements are reviewed with the buyer. Same lab partners, same actives, same heavy metals and microbiological panel, your label up top. Helps brands that want clean white-label traceability without exposing the upstream supplier.

Capsule-ready, gummy-ready, stick-pack-ready

Our private-label mushroom powders are spray-dried to mesh 80–120, free-flowing, low static, and validated against the standard formats finished-product brands fill in. Capsule-ready — bulk density 0.45–0.55 g/ml, suitable for size 0 and size 00 vegetarian and gelatine capsules. Gummy-ready — soluble fraction validated, no clumping, compatible with pectin and gelatin gummy bases. Stick-pack-ready — fast dispersion, neutral taste profile on dual-extract lots, validated for stick-pack and sachet fill machines.

Packaging — bulk or your retail format

Default packaging is food-grade foil-laminated drums (5, 10 or 25 kg) with batch traceability sticker. For private-label retail buyers we can supply in your branded pouches, jars or stick-packs direct from our HACCP-aligned facility — supply us the artwork and packaging spec and we drop the finished lot ready for your warehouse. Most private-label brands run a hybrid: bulk drums for their own downstream filling, with retail-ready packs from us on their fastest-moving SKUs.

MOQ, lead time and standing contracts

Custom private-label blend MOQ is 25 kg on the first PO (single SKU). Single-species mushroom powder MOQ is 5–25 kg depending on species. Lead time on a first PO is typically 4–6 weeks from spec confirmation (blend formulation, sample approval, production, COA, shipping). Standing contracts cut lead time to 2–3 weeks because lots are pre-allocated against your forecast. Most private-label brands move to a standing quarterly or monthly contract after their second order.
