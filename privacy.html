Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: 3 April 2026  |  Effective date: 3 April 2026

1. Who We Are

Good Mushroom ("we", "our", "us") is a mushroom trading and export company based in Himachal Pradesh, India. We operate the website goodmushroom.in (the "Site"). You can reach us at krish@goodmushroom.in.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact form submissions — When you fill out our buyer or seller enquiry forms, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, country, and the message you send us.
  • Usage data — We may collect non-personally identifiable information about how you use the Site, including pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs, through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communications — If you email us directly, we keep a record of that correspondence.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as financial information, government ID numbers, or health data).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you requested
  • Send you information about our products and pricing (only if you requested it)
  • Improve and maintain the Site
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Our Site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. We use the following types:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the Site to function (e.g., remembering your form state).
  • Analytics cookies — We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. This collects anonymous data such as pages visited and session duration. Google Analytics uses its own cookies. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Advertising cookies — We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

5. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display ads on our Site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visits to this and other websites to provide relevant advertisements.

Google's advertising requirements can be summarised by Google's Advertising Principles. They are put in place to provide a positive experience for users. For more information on how Google uses data when you use our Site, visit policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

We have no access to or control over the cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

6. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Email service providers — Your form submissions are sent to our team email addresses (krish@goodmushroom.in and anmol@goodmushroom.in) via your email client (mailto).
  • Google — For analytics and advertising purposes as described above.
  • Legal authorities — If required by law, court order, or government request.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data submitted through our contact forms for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain a business relationship, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of personalised ads — Manage your ad preferences via Google Ads Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at krish@goodmushroom.in.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Links to Other Websites

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: krish@goodmushroom.in
  • Address: Himachal Pradesh, India
