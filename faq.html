Frequently asked questions

What we supply, how quality evidence is handled, how supplier review works and what to expect before a quote.

Good Mushroom Private Limited is an India-based mushroom trading and sourcing company. We work with growing partners and coordinate product review, buyer specifications and route-specific documentation for B2B ingredient buyers.
MOQ depends on species, format, grade and the buyer route. Request a Quote with your required format and destination so we can confirm the current commercial minimum.
We are building route-specific supply for international B2B ingredient buyers. Destination availability, documentation and freight are confirmed for the specific product and buyer route.
Where testing is commissioned for the product route, we can share an accredited partner-lab Certificate of Analysis (COA). Testing scope is matched to the product and buyer specification.
FSSAI, APEDA and AYUSH registrations remain in process. Destination-specific phytosanitary and export documentation is assessed for the relevant shipment route. Any optional third-party certification is confirmed with the buyer before quoting.
Paid samples can be discussed based on the product, format and destination. Sample testing and documentation are confirmed for the specific route; request a quote to confirm availability and freight.
Use the supplier form or WhatsApp the team with your location, species, monthly volume and current selling channel. We review fit first, and purchase terms are agreed in writing only when the product and current demand match.
Farm size is not the only consideration. We look at species, handling, quality evidence, location, volume consistency and current buyer requirements.
Purchase terms are agreed in writing after reviewing the product, quality grade, location, volume and current demand. Use the supplier form to start the review.
